Serve With Us

Use your gifts and talents to make a difference. There's a place for you to serve!

👋

Ushering Team

Help create a welcoming environment by greeting guests, handing out programs, and assisting with seating during services.

Time Commitment: One Sunday per month

Requirements: Friendly smile and servant heart

🎵

Choir & Worship

Use your musical gifts to lead the congregation in worship. Vocalists and instrumentalists welcome!

Time Commitment: Weekly rehearsals + Sunday services

Requirements: Musical ability and heart for worship

📹

Media Team

Help with live streaming, sound, lighting, and presentation slides during services. No experience needed - we'll train you!

Time Commitment: One Sunday per month

Requirements: Willingness to learn technical skills

👶

Children's Ministry

Invest in the next generation by teaching and caring for children during Sunday services. Background check required.

Time Commitment: One Sunday per month

Requirements: Love for children and patience

🌍

Outreach Team

Help us serve our community through various outreach projects, food distribution, and evangelism efforts.

Time Commitment: As needed for events

Requirements: Heart for serving others

☕

Café Team

Prepare and serve coffee/tea before and after services. Help create a warm, welcoming atmosphere for fellowship.

Time Commitment: One Sunday per month

Requirements: Friendly and service-oriented
