About Our Ministry

Discover our story, mission, and the heart behind Heavenly Glory Revelation Ministries

Our Story

Founded in Faith, Growing in Grace

Heavenly Glory Revelation Ministries began in 2010 with a small group of passionate believers who gathered in a living room, hungry for a deeper revelation of God's glory. What started as a humble prayer meeting has blossomed into a thriving community of faith.

Over the past decade, we've witnessed countless lives transformed, families restored, and destinies fulfilled through the power of God's word and the presence of the Holy Spirit. Our journey has been marked by faith, perseverance, and an unwavering commitment to God's calling.

Today, we continue to build on the foundation laid by our founders, reaching new generations with the timeless message of God's love and the revelation of His heavenly glory.

Our Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To reveal God's heavenly glory through passionate worship, biblical teaching, and compassionate service to our community. We are called to be a lighthouse of hope, guiding people to encounter the transforming power of God's presence.

  • Worship God with passion and authenticity
  • Teach biblical truth with clarity and relevance
  • Serve our community with love and compassion
  • Make disciples who make disciples

👁️ Our Vision

To be a thriving spiritual home where lives are transformed, faith is strengthened, and God's love is shared with all. We envision a community where every person encounters God's glory and discovers their divine purpose.

  • A church filled with God's presence and power
  • A community transformed by God's love
  • Generations walking in divine destiny
  • A beacon of hope to our city and beyond

What We Believe

📜 The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and the ultimate authority for our faith and life.

✝️ Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is the Son of God, fully divine and fully human, who died for our sins and rose again for our salvation.

🕊️ The Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit is the third person of the Trinity who empowers believers, guides the church, and convicts the world.

⛪ The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, called to worship God, make disciples, and serve the world until His return.
